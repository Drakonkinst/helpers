Dictionary/Vocabulary Helper

This script will open inputted words in an online dictionary. Useful for searching mass amounts of vocabulary words without dying in the process.

WARNING : You may need to enable popups for this website to function correctly, as opening another window is considered a popup.

This site is a work in progress, and may not function to a suitable quality level. Use at your own discretion.

Select a dictionary to use. Merraim-Webster is detailed, Ninjawords is fast, and Dictionary.com makes for a nice medium.


Enter your words here, separated by spaces. (ex: one two three four) Press Submit or the ENTER key to search the words.

Note: Using Dictionary.com or Merraim-Webster for multiple words will open multiple tabs in your browser.


Alternatively, you can choose to import a word list from the following selections. They are only really applicable in certain situations.
