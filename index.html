Dictionary/Vocabulary Helper

This script will open inputted words in an online dictionary. Useful for searching mass amounts of vocabulary words without dying in the process.

WARNING : A computer or laptop is recommended for using this website. Phones and other mobile devices may not work as well.

TROUBLESHOOTING : If only one window shows up after entering multiple words, ensure that pop-ups are disabled for this website.

This site is a work in progress and may not perform to a suitable quality level. Use at your own discretion.

Select a dictionary to use. Merriam-Webster is detailed, Ninjawords is fast, and Dictionary.com makes for a nice medium.


Enter your words here, separated by spaces. (ex: one two three four) Press Submit or the ↵ ENTER key to search the words.

Warning : Using Dictionary.com or Merriam-Webster for multiple words will open multiple tabs in your browser. It may be a good idea to search only a few words at a time.

Alternatively, you can choose to import a word list from the following selections. They are only really applicable in certain situations.
